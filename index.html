Village Description

Dalangan is a traditional village located in Blora, Central Java, known for its rich culture, rice fields, and strong community bonds. This village is surrounded by green landscapes and features periodic festivals and local craftsmanship.

Map & Geography

Located in Blora, Central Java, Dalangan is surrounded by lush landscapes and is shaped by a tropical monsoon climate with rich agricultural resources.
